Chocolate Coconut Pudding Pie

This is a chocolate pudding pie, that happens to be dairy free (that does not mean it's healthy though...). Adapted from this recipe.

Ingredients
- Blind baked pie crust of your choice, cooled
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 c sugar
- ⅓ c cocoa powder, sifted
- ⅓ c AP flour, sifted
- ¼ tsp salt
- 1 13.5 oz can coconut milk
- 2 Tbs coconut oil
Instructions
- In a medium saucepan on medium heat, whisk the egg yolks and sugar until they are no longer grainy.
- Stir in the flour and cocoa powder. It will be thiick so you may want to switch to a spoon.
- Gradually whisk in the coconut milk, and bring to a boil whisking continuously. It'll be thin at first but will magically thicken. Any lumps should work themselves out.
- Boil, stirring constantly for 1 minute.
- Pour into prepared crust, being certain not to scrape the saucepan too well.
- Work the remaining pudding out of the pan with your tongue.
- Cover the pie and chill it. Either cover with plastic wrap or be careful when removing the cover to avoid dripping condensation.
